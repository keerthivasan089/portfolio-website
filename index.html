The Story Behind the Data

I'm Keerthi Vasan P, a 3rd-year Computer Science Engineering student at Adhiyamaan College of Engineering, Hosur, with a CGPA of 8.0.

My journey into data started with a simple curiosity: why do businesses make the decisions they make? I soon discovered that the answer always lies in data. From there, I dove into SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Python — not just to learn tools, but to understand how data drives real business outcomes.

I've interned at UV Netware Private Limited as a Database Analyst and currently work as a Data Analyst Intern at LocalSM, where I analyze real product and user behavior data to support business decisions.

My long-term goal is to become a Data Engineer — building scalable data pipelines and systems that power large-scale analytics. I document my learning journey as part of a 100 Days of Data Analyst series on LinkedIn.
